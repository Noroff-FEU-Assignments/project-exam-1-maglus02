About Us

Quantum Realm is a blog covering the latest and greatest within tech ang gaming. We strive to deliver quality tech and gaming content to all our readers.

Quantum Realm, was created back in 2009 as a passion project. We felt like combining tech and gaming was one of the greatest things we did. It’s a great duo when creating a blog. As both of them can intertwine often.

Our goal with this blog is to deliver at least one new quality post for our readers to read every single day, usually more.
